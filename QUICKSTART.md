# Quick Start Guide - Online Gift Shop

## 🚀 Getting Started in 3 Steps

### Step 1: Login or Register

When you first open the application, you'll see the authentication page.

**Option A - Use Demo Admin Account:**
- Email: `admin@giftshop.com`
- Password: `admin123`
- This gives you access to the Admin Panel

**Option B - Create New Account:**
- Click on the "Register" tab
- Fill in:
  - Full Name
  - Email (any valid email format)
  - Mobile Number (10 digits)
  - Password (minimum 6 characters)
- Click "Register"

### Step 2: Explore the Shop

Once logged in, you can:

1. **Browse Products** - Click "Products" in the navigation
2. **Search & Filter** - Use the search bar and filters
3. **View Details** - Click on any product card
4. **Customize Gifts** - Select products marked "Customizable"
5. **Add to Cart** - Click "Add to Cart" button
6. **Add to Wishlist** - Click the heart icon

### Step 3: Complete Purchase

1. **View Cart** - Click the cart icon (shows item count)
2. **Apply Coupon** - Try these codes:
   - `GIFT10` - Get 10% off
   - `SAVE20` - Get 20% off
   - `FIRST50` - Get ₹50 off
3. **Checkout** - Click "Proceed to Checkout"
4. **Fill Details** - Enter delivery address
5. **Select Payment** - Choose payment method
6. **Place Order** - Complete your order
7. **Confirmation** - View order details and download invoice

## 🎨 Product Customization

For customizable products:

1. Click "Customize & Add" button
2. Add custom text (e.g., "Happy Birthday!")
3. Upload an image (optional)
4. Select color and size (if available)
5. Preview your customization
6. Note: +20% fee applies to customized items

## 🛍️ Shopping Tips

- **Free Delivery**: Orders above ₹999 ship free!
- **Price Range**: Gifts from ₹349 to ₹2,499
- **Categories**: Browse by Birthday, Anniversary, Corporate, Festival, or Customized gifts
- **Ratings**: Check customer ratings before buying
- **Discounts**: Look for discount badges (up to 38% off!)

## 👨‍💼 Admin Panel (Admin Only)

If logged in as admin, you can:

1. Click "Admin Panel" in the header menu
2. View dashboard statistics
3. Manage orders - Update order status
4. View all products - Edit or delete products
5. See registered users
